# 014 Demo Adventure - Design

## Premise

A village lantern that protects the hill road has gone dark. Travelers report pale lights beneath the old hill. The mayor asks the party to inspect the lantern before the next storm.

DM-only truth:

The lantern was built above a sealed shrine. A frightened apprentice broke the seal while searching for a missing sibling. Goblin scavengers found the opening, and an old spirit is using the lantern light to lure help.

## Scenes

Suggested scenes:

1. Village square
2. Mayor's house
3. Old hill road
4. Lantern tower
5. Buried shrine
6. Ending scene

## NPCs

- Mayor Elric: nervous village leader, hiding old records.
- Mira: apprentice who broke the seal.
- Old Kett: road keeper who knows the lantern's history.
- Whisper in the Flame: bound spirit beneath the hill.

## Clues

- Muddy small footprints near the tower.
- Broken lantern lens with strange soot.
- Old record mentioning a sealed shrine.
- Mira's dropped charm.
- Whispered phrase from the flame.
- Goblin scrap map.
- Symbol under the tower hatch.

## Encounter

A simple encounter at the lantern tower or shrine:

- 2 goblin-like scavengers or SRD goblins if allowed by fixture policy.
- Optional environmental hazard: unstable stones.

## Endings

- Repair the lantern and reseal the shrine.
- Free the bound spirit safely.
- Bargain with the spirit.
- Fail to repair the lantern before the storm.

## Fixture format

The demo should first be authored as structured Markdown.

Later, tests should parse it into AdventureModule JSON.

## Design purpose

This adventure is not meant to be a masterpiece. It is meant to exercise:

- public read-aloud text;
- DM-only truth;
- clue reveal;
- NPC interaction;
- simple combat;
- scene transition;
- recap.
